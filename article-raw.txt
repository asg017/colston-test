'''Marjorie Greene''' is an American politician from the [[U.S. state|state]] of [[Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia]]. She is a [[Republican Party (United States)|Republican]] candidate in the [[2020 United States House of Representatives elections in Georgia|2020 elections]] seeking to represent {{ushr|GA|14}} in the [[United States House of Representatives]].

In the days before the primary election, [[Facebook]] took down a Greene advertisement in which she brandished an [[AR-15]].<ref>https://www.timesfreepress.com/news/local/story/2020/jun/05/facebook-deletes-marjorie-greene-ad-inciting/524666/</ref>
