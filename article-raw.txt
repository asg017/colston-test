On August 23, 2020, '''Jacob Blake''', a 29-year-old black man, was shot from behind seven times by a police officer in [[Kenosha, Wisconsin]].<ref name="thedailybeast">{{cite web |last1=Connor |first1=Tracy |title=Wisconsin Police Shoot Black Man as Heâ€™s Getting in Car |url=https://www.thedailybeast.com/kenosha-wisconsin-police-shoot-black-man-as-hes-getting-in-car |publisher=[[The Daily Beast]] |date=August 24, 2020}}</ref><ref name="thewashingtonpost">{{cite web |last1=Peiser |first1=Jaclyn |title=Video shows Wisconsin police shooting a Black man multiple times as he enters a car |url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/nation/2020/08/23/kenosha-police-shooting-video-wisconsin/ |publisher=[[The Washington Post]] |date=August 24, 2020}}</ref> Protests occurred in response, as part of the larger [[Black Lives Matter]] movement which had seen a resurgence in the wake of high-profile police killings in 2020, including [[Killing of George Floyd|George Floyd]] and [[Breonna Taylor]].

==Incident==
Witnesses alleged that Blake had been attempting to break up a fight between two women. It is unknown what led to the police interacting with Blake, but it has been reported that they attempted to subdue him with a [[taser]].<ref name="thewashingtonpost"/><ref name="npr"/> Video on social media showed Blake surrounded by police with their weapons drawn. Blake ignored their demands, walked around his vehicle and attempted to enter it. As he did, one police officer grabbed him and fired his weapon into Blake's back.  The ''[[Kenosha News]]'' reported "it was not immediately known whether the man had a weapon".<ref>{{cite news | last = Flores | first = Terry | date = August 23, 2020 | title = WATCH NOW: State DOJ Will Probe Officer-Involved Shooting; Man In Serious Condition | url = https://www.kenoshanews.com/news/local/watch-now-neighbors-man-shot-by-police-at-40th-street-and-28th-ave/article_f1adbd13-3a15-5cdd-bd4e-95113ca36de4.html#tracking-source=home-top-story | work = Kenosha News}}</ref>  Blake's attorney later said that Blake's three sons were inside the vehicle at the time.<ref>{{cite news|first=Alta|last=Spells|url=https://edition.cnn.com/2020/08/24/us/kenosha-police-shooting-jacob-blake/index.html |title=Wisconsin police shoot Black man as children watch from a car, attorney says |date=August 24, 2020|publisher=[[CNN]]|department=US}}</ref> Blake was left in serious condition and flown to a hospital.<ref name="npr">{{cite news|url=https://www.npr.org/2020/08/24/905316709/wisconsin-police-shooting-leaves-black-man-in-serious-condition |title=Wisconsin Police Shooting Leaves Black Man In 'Serious Condition' |date=August 24, 2020|publisher=[[NPR]]|first=Emma|last=Bowman}}</ref>

==Reactions==
Wisconsin Governor [[Tony Evers]] issued a statement denouncing the [[Police brutality in the United States|excessive use of force by police]] and invoking the names of African Americans killed by law enforcement.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.cbs58.com/news/gov-evers-releases-statement-on-shooting-of-jacob-blake-in-kenosha |title=Gov. Evers releases statement on shooting of Jacob Blake in Kenosha |date=August 23, 2020| publisher=[[WDJT-TV]]}}</ref> Evers stated that "While we do not have all of the details yet, what we know for certain is that he is not the first black man or person to have been shot or injured or mercilessly killed at the hands of individuals in law enforcement in our state or our country."<ref name=erupt>{{cite news|url= https://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-53886070|work=[[BBC News]]|department=US & Canada|title=Kenosha shooting: Protests erupt after US police shoot black man|date=August 24, 2020}}</ref>

Protests followed, leading the [[Kenosha County, Wisconsin|Kenosha County]] government to declare a [[state of emergency]] overnight on August 24.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2020/aug/24/protests-in-wisconsin-after-video-appears-to-show-police-shooting-black-man-in-the-back |title=Protests in Wisconsin after video appears to show police shooting black man in the back|date=August 24, 2020|publisher=[[The Guardian]]}}</ref> Police vehicles were damaged and a [[dump truck]] was set on fire. One officer was hit in the head with a flying object. [[Tear gas]] was deployed by the police.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://jsonline.com/story/news/2020/08/24/kenosha-protests-escalate-after-police-shoot-black-man-jacob-blake/3427941001/ |title=Several fires burning in Kenosha as unrest continues after police officer shoots Black man |date=August 24, 2020|publisher= [[Milwaukee Journal Sentinel]]}}</ref><ref name="npr"/> Police urged 24-hour businesses to consider closing because of what it alleged were "numerous" calls about armed robberies and shots being fired.<ref name=erupt/>
