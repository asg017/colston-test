'''Marjorie Taylor Greene''' is an American politician and businesswoman from the [[U.S. state|state]] of [[Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia]]. She is a [[Republican Party (United States)|Republican]] candidate in the [[2020 United States House of Representatives elections in Georgia|2020 elections]] seeking to represent {{ushr|GA|14}} in the [[United States House of Representatives]].

Greene graduated from the [[University of Georgia]].
